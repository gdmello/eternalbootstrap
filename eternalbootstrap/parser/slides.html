Jesus falls the third time
12:00pm
Clement Jesus, I offer you infinite thanks for not permitting me an ungrateful sinner to die in my sins, as you have permitted thousands less criminal, to die in their sins. I, who have added torments to your torments by heaping sin on sin, kindle in my soul the fire of charity, fan it with your continual grace into perseverance, until delivered from this body of death that I can enjoy the liberty with the children of God and your coheirs. Amen.
Fruit- To frequent the sacrament of confession.
Jesus washes the feet of his apostles
8:00pm
O my Jesus! By that humiliation which you practised in washing the feet of your disciples, I pray you to bestow upon me the grace of true humility, that I may humble myself to all, especially to those who treat me with contempt.
Fruit- For the grace to serve others.
Jesus consoles the women
11:15am
Jesus, only begotten Son of the Father, who will give water to my head, and a fountain of tears to my eyes, that I may, day and night weep and lament my sins? I humbly beseech you by these tears of blood that you shed for me, to grant me true contrition that will soften my hardened heart, cancel my crimes and render me secure in the day of wrath and examination, when you will come to judge the living and the dead, and demand a rigorous account of your blood. Amen.
Fruit- For a contrite heart and sincere repentance.
Jesus is led before Annas
11:00pm
My Jesus, by that pain which you did feel at seeing yourself bound like a culprit to be taken before the judges, I pray you to bind me to yourself by the sweet chains of holy love, that I may never more see myself separated from you, my only good.
Fruit- Grace to deny myself & take up the yoke of Christ.
Jesus Carries the Cross
6:45am
Meek and humble Jesus, my iniquity and perverseness loaded your shoulders with the heavy burden of the cross. Yet I flee even the appearance of mortification and everything which would check the violence of my passions and if I suffered, it was with a murmuring reluctance. I now, O Savior of the world detest my wrongs, and by your grace I am determined no more to offend you by my sins. Let me only glory in the cross of my Lord by whom the world is crucified to me, and I to the world. Lay then on my stubborn neck the cross of true penance; let me for the love of you bear the adversities of this life, and cling inseparably to you in the bonds of perpetual love. Amen.
Fruit- Help me not to flee from pain or waver when faced with trials.
Jesus is pierced with a lance
3:45pm
My Jesus, Alas!!! I do not stop with the crucifixion but further pierce you with my words, actions and my disbelief. I cause you more pain upon injury. I sometimes follow others blindly even if I am convinced that what they are saying and doing are wrong. Help me to stand up for my beliefs and not be afraid of it.
Fruit- To believe & receive the sacraments of the church.
Jesus meets His Mother
8:15am
Mary, I am the cause of your sufferings. O Refuge of Sinners let me participate in those heart-felt pangs which rent your tender soul, when you did behold your son trembling with cold, covered with wounds, fainting under the cross, more dead than alive. Mournful Mother, Fountain of Love, let me feel the force of your grief that I may weep with you, mingle my tears with your tears and your Son's blood. O suffering Jesus, by your bitter passion, and the heart-breaking compassion of your afflicted Mother, grant me the efficacious grace of perseverance. Mother of Jesus, intercede for me. Jesus, behold me with an eye of pity, and in the hour of my death receive me into the arms of your mercy. Amen.
Fruit- Grace to empathize with those who are suffering and extend a helping hand.
Jesus is taken before Caiphas and receives a blow in the face
11:30pm
My sweetest Jesus, declared by the council of the Jews guilty of death, and in the house of Caiaphas blindfolded with a piece of cloth, and then buffeted, spat at, and derided, have mercy on us.
Fruit- Grace to endure all forms of injustice.
Jesus is taken down from the cross
4:30pm
At length, O Blessed Virgin, Mother of sorrow, you are permitted to embrace your beloved Son. But alas, the fruit of your immaculate womb is mangled into one continuous wound. Yes O Lord, the fury of the Jewish leaders has at length triumphed, yet we renew their barbarity crucifying you by our sins inflicting new wounds. Most afflicted Mother of my Redeemer, I ask you by the pains and torments you suffered because of salvation, to obtain for me by your powerful intercession, pardon for my sins and grace to sympathize with you and your Son's afflictions. As often as I appear at the Holy Sacrifice of the mass, let me embrace you my Jesus, in the bosom of my heart. May I worthily receive you as the sacred pledge of my salvation. Amen.
Fruit- To tenderly love & honor the Mother of our Lord.
The resurrection of Jesus
6:30pm
Ah, my God, I deserve not paradise, but hell; yet Your death gives me a hope of obtaining it. I desire and ask paradise of You, not so much in order to enjoy, as in order to love You everlastingly. O Mary, my Mother, O Star of the Sea, it is for you, by your prayers, to conduct me to paradise.
Fruit- To be hopeful in all situations, no matter how bleak we may perceive it to be.
Jesus is led to the council and declared guilty of death
12:00 am
My Jesus, by all those insults, buffetings, and spitting which you on that night suffered in the house of Caiaphas, give me the strength to suffer in peace for love of you all the affronts which I shall meet with from men.
Fruit- Grace not to retaliate when offended.
Jesus institutes the Most Holy Sacrament of the Eucharist
8:30pm
My most loving Jesus, as I receive your body, renew me with this Eucharist, draw me ever close to your Heart. Help me profess my faith in your blood through all I do.
Fruit- Transform my whole life into a Eucharist for your glory.
The sweating of blood
10:00pm
My beloved Jesus, so great were my sins that you experienced excruciating pain to the point of sweating blood. Pardon me my Lord and grant me the grace to surrender every sin & even the desire to sin.
Fruit- To be willing to die to my sin rather than to offend Jesus.
Jesus is conducted to Pilate, and Barabbas is preferred to him
3:45am
My Jesus, by that outrage which you did receive from the Jews in seeing yourself placed after Barabbas, give me the grace to suffer with patience the dishonor of seeing myself placed after others.
Fruit- To make right judgments between good & evil.
Jesus is mocked by Herod
3:00am
My Jesus, by that ridicule which you received from Herod in being treated as a fool, give me the grace to endure with patience all that men may say to me or treat me as senseless.
Fruit- To be a fool for Christ when ridiculed for our beliefs, morals & values.
Jesus celebrates his last supper with his disciples
7:30pm
O my Jesus, do you desire so ardently to give your life for us your miserable creatures? Ah! This desire inflames our hearts with a desire to suffer and die for the love of you, since you chose to suffer and die for the love of us. O beloved Redeemer, make known to us what you will from us. We desire to please you, to correspond at least in part to your great love for us. Increase always this blessed flame within us. May it make us forget the world and ourselves, that from this day forward we may think only of pleasing your enamored heart.
Fruit- For the grace to accept suffering & deny myself.
Jesus falls the second time
10:30am
Most Holy Redeemer treated with the utmost contempt, deprived of fame and honor, led out to punishment through excess of torments, and the weakness of your mangled body, did fall a second time to the earth. What impious hand has prostrated you? Alas, my Jesus, I am that impious, that sacrilegious offender. My ambitious pride, my haughty indignation and my contempt of others humbled you to the earth. Banish for ever from my mind the spirit of pride. Teach my heart the doctrine of humility, so that detesting pride, vain glory and human respect, I may for ever be united with you my meek and humble Jesus. Amen.
Fruit- For the grace not to be proud & haughty.
Jesus prays for his murderers
2:15pm
My Jesus, how many times I have daily nailed you to the cross by not believing that you are the Lord in all situations of my life. Please pardon me for all the wrong that I continue to do every moment of the day. I pray that you may give me the mind of Christ so that I may be ever mindful of my thoughts, actions, choices and their impact on those around me.
Fruit- To acknowledge that Jesus is Lord & that salvation comes through Him.
Peter denies Jesus
1:30am
My beloved Jesus, I continue to deny you daily by my thoughtless words & actions. Grant me the grace to take every thought captive, to put on the mind of Christ and never to deny your friendship to suit my convenience.
Fruit- Grace to submit my thoughts & mind to the lordship of Jesus.
Jesus is nailed to the Cross
1:30pm
Patient Jesus, meek Lamb of God, who promised, "When I shall be exalted from earth I will draw all things to myself," attract my heart to you, and nail it to the cross. I now renounce and detest my impatience. Let me crucify my flesh with its concupiscence and vices. Burn and cut away my wrong, and spare me for eternity. I throw myself into the arms of your mercy. Your will be done in all things. Grant me resignation, grant me your love, I desire no more. Amen.
Fruit- The grace to accept our cross willingly.
Jesus is scourged at the pillar
4:30am
My Jesus, by that pain which you did suffer in your most holy body when you were so cruelly scourged, give me the grace to suffer with patience all the pain that I may experience as a result of sicknesses, and the type of death destined for me.
Fruit- To attain perfect knowledge through suffering & to suffer rightly.
Jesus is blindfolded, struck, and scoffed at
12:45am
My dearest Jesus, help us not to blindly follow those around us even when we know they are wrong. Help us to stand up for the truth even if it means being unpopular or unaccepted, or if it means to die for the truth.
Fruit- For the grace to be courageous.
Jesus is delivered over to his Mother
5:15pm
May we too, draw near the body of Jesus, lowered from the cross and held in his Mother's arms. Let us approach then not by walking but by believing, not with physical steps but with the free decision of our heart. In this lifeless body we see ourselves as members of Christ, his limbs, wounded and suffering, yet safe in the loving embrace of our Mother. Let us also see ourselves as held in her motherly arms, firm yet gentle the very image of our Mother the church where we become the mystical body of Christ. As we contemplate the figure of the Pieta we learn devotion to the "yes" of love; we learn surrender and acceptance, trust and practical concern, a tender heart which restores life and awakens joy.
Fruit- Acceptance of God's will in my life.
Jesus is crowned with the thorns, and exhibited to the people
5:15am
My Jesus, by that pain which you did suffer in your most sacred head when it was pierced with thorns, give me the grace never to consent to thoughts that are displeasing to you.
Fruit- For my thoughts to be pure & holy and to bring it under the Lordship of Jesus.
Jesus is taken to Pilate and accused
2:15am
My sweetest Jesus, led away as a malefactor to Pilate, and then turned by Herod into ridicule, and treated as a madman, have mercy on us.
Fruit- For the grace to be tuned to the promptings of the Holy Spirit regarding the truth.
Jesus commends his spirit to the Father & Dies on the cross
3:00pm
Suffering Son of God, I now behold you in the last convulsive pangs of death. Your veins opened, your sinews torn, your hands and feet bleeding. I acknowledge charitable Jesus, that my reiterated offenses have been your merciless executioners, the cause of your bitter sufferings and death. Yet God of mercy, look on my sinful soul, and bathe it in your precious blood. Let me die to the vanity of the world and renounce its false pleasures. You did pray my Jesus for your enemies. I forgive mine. I embrace them in the depths of your charity. I bury my resentment in your wounds. Shelter me in the day of wrath in the sanctuary of your side. Let me live, let me die, in my crucified Jesus.
Fruit- Total submission to the Father's will in our lives.
Jesus is led to the council and declared guilty of death
12:00 am
My Jesus, by all those insults, buffetings, and spitting which you on that night suffered in the house of Caiaphas, give me the strength to suffer in peace for love of you all the affronts which I shall meet with from men.
Fruit- Grace not to retaliate when offended.
Veronica wipes the face of Jesus
9:45am
Jesus, grant me tears to weep my ingratitude. How often have I infatuated wretch, turned my eyes from you and your sufferings to fix them on the world and its vanities. Let me henceforth be yours without division. Stamp your image on my soul that it may never admit another love. Take possession of my heart on earth that my soul may take eternal possession of you in glory.
Fruit- To be able to see the need & brokenness in the lives of others and to respond.
Jesus is stripped of His garments
12:45pm
Suffering Jesus, I behold you stripped of your garments, your old wounds renewed, and new ones added. I behold you naked in the presence of thousands exposed to the inclemency of the weather, cold and trembling from head to foot, insulted by the blasphemous derision of the spectators. Strip, O mangled Lamb of God, my heart of attachments to the world and its deceitful affections. Protect my soul from its habits of sensual indulgence. Let me give up the cup of pleasure that I may dash it with contempt from my lips, and through Christian mortification arrive at your never fading glory. Amen.
Fruit- For the gift of contentment especially in regards to food, clothing & other wants.
Jesus is condemned to death
6:00am
Mangled victim of my sins, O suffering Jesus! I have deserved those bloody scourges, that cruel sentence of death, and yet, you did die for me that I should live for you. I am convinced that if I desire to please men, I cannot be your servant. Let me then displease the world and its vain admirers. I resign myself into your hands. Let love take possession of my heart. Let my eyes behold with contempt everything that could alienate my affections from you; let my ears be ever attentive to your word; let me through this painful journey accompany you. Jesus have mercy.
Fruit- To die to ourselves in order to merit heavenly glory.
Jesus is betrayed by Judas and is bound
10:30pm
My Jesus, by that pain which you did experience at seeing yourself betrayed by Judas with a kiss, give me the grace to be ever faithful to you, and never more to betray you, as I have done in time past.
Fruit- Grace to be a true friend of Jesus.
Jesus is helped by Simon of Cyrene
9:00am
O suffering Jesus to what excess did your impious executioners' cruelty proceed? Beholding you faint under the cross, apprehensive of your death before they could complete their bloody intentions, they compel Simon to carry the cross that you might expire on it in the most exquisite torture. Should I complain of their cruelty or the repugnance of Simon? Have I not again crucified you by my crimes? Have I not suffered with fretful impatience the light afflictions with which your mercy visited me? Inspire me now, my Jesus, to detest and deplore my sinful impatience, ungrateful murmurs, and let me with all my heart cheerfully accompany you to Mount Calvary; let me live in you and die in you. Amen.
Fruit- Willingness to accept the crosses in my life.
Agony in the garden of Gethsemane
9:30pm
My Jesus, by that horror which you had of my sins, which were then present to your sight, give me a true sorrow for all the offences which I have committed against you.
Fruit- Grace to experience deep sorrow for offending Jesus.
Jesus is laid in the sepulchre
6:00pm
Charitable Jesus, for my salvation you performed the painful journey of the cross. Let me follow the footsteps marked by you O gracious Redeemer, the paths which through the thorns of life conduct to the heavenly Jerusalem. Would that you were entombed in my heart, that being united to you, I might rise to a new life of grace and persevere to the end. Grant me in my last moments to receive your precious body, as the pledge of immortal life. Let my last words be Jesus and Mary, my last breath be united to your last breath on the cross, that with a lively faith, a firm hope, and ardent love, I may die with you and for you, that I may reign with you for ever and ever. Amen.
Fruit- To reverence our Lord where he lies hidden in the blessed sacrament.
Jesus, takes leave of Mary
7:00pm
My Jesus, by that pain which You experienced in taking leave of Your holy Mother, deliver me from an inordinate love for my relatives, or for any other creature, so that my heart may be wholly and always Yours.
Fruit- For the grace of detachment.
Jesus falls the first time
7:30am
Jesus, the merciless violence of your executioners, the weight of the cross, or rather the more oppressive load of my sins, crush you to the earth. Panting for breath, exhausted as you are, you did not refuse to endure new tortures for me. Will I then refuse the light burden of your commandments; will I refuse to do violence to my perverse passions and sinful attachments; will I relapse into those very crimes for which I have shed false and delusive tears? O Jesus stretch your holy hand to my assistance, that I may never more fall into mortal sin; that I may at the hour of death secure my salvation. Amen.
Fruit- Grace to be free from sliding back into mortal sin.
Prayer of Jesus in the garden
9:00pm
My Jesus, by that sorrow which you suffered in the garden, sufficient as it was to cause your death, I pray you to deliver me from the sorrow of hell, from living evermore at a distance from you, and without the power of ever loving you again.
Fruit- Grace to draw strength from Jesus in difficult situations of our lives.
